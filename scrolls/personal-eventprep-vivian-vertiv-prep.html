Chapter Four

Your Conversation Playbook

When They Ask
"What do you know about Vertiv?"
Tell the story: Liebert heritage, Emerson spin-off, AI-driven inflection point. Mention the Delaware microgrid and BYOP vision. Show you understand this isn't just a power company—it's the infrastructure backbone of the AI revolution. Stats to back you up: $10.2B revenue, $15B backlog, 2.9x book-to-bill. But lead with the narrative.
When They Ask
"Why project management? Why here?"
Connect the velocity of innovation—fuel cell UPS integration, EnergyCore battery cabinets, microgrid controllers—to your ability to keep complex cross-functional programs on track. You thrive where stakes are real and pace is fast. Delaware isn't just a campus. It's where the products powering the next decade of data centers are born.
When They Ask
"How do you handle a project falling behind?"
Vertiv values "Act With Urgency" and "Own It." Root-cause fast, rally the right people, communicate transparently, build a recovery plan with clear milestones. Don't hide problems. Have a specific example ready.
Tailored Questions for Each Session
Pick 1–2 per person. Quality over quantity.
Todd S. & Dominic (peers): "What does a typical week look like for a PM here? How many active programs are you each running?" • "Where do NPD projects tend to hit the most friction?"

Ben (Sr. Director): "How is the PM function evolving as the backlog accelerates—process, headcount, or both?" • "What does success look like in the first 90 days?"

Todd Marchal (VP): "Where is the Power Solutions roadmap heading, and what are the biggest priorities the PM team needs to support?" • "How do you balance time-to-market urgency against getting the product right?"

Milind (VP): "The EnergyCore cabinet launch was impressive. How did the PM team support that from development to market?" • "How does energy storage connect to the broader BYOP and microgrid vision?"
