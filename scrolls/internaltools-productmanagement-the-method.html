Playbook

What the Top Sellers Know

  • 01
    Niche beats general, every time. "Paycheck-by-Paycheck Budget Planner for Nurses Working 3×12 Shifts" converts 3–5× better than a generic "budget planner."
  • 02
    Bundles outsell singles 3×. The hub-and-spoke model — one core product with 5–8 add-ons at a bundle discount — generates far more revenue per customer.
  • 03
    Design matters more than function. One seller built a $280K business by making budget spreadsheets pink and modern instead of the default greens and blues.
  • 04
    Seasonal timing is everything. January resolutions, August back-to-school, March Madness, tax season — timing your launch to demand spikes is a multiplier.
  • 05
    Free tier → paid upsell. Give away basic, collect emails, sell premium. The growth engine that works across every template platform.
  • 06
    Video demos convert. A 15-second screen recording of data entry → chart update outperforms any written listing description.

Go-to-Market

Distribution in 5 Phases

Discovery is the bottleneck, not product quality. Here's the playbook:

Marketplace Seeding

List free / low-cost on Etsy, Gumroad, Product Hunt. $0.20 listing fee beats building an audience from scratch.

Viral Group Shares

Guerrilla tools spread via group chats. One bracket in 50 friend groups = 500+ potential buyers.

Content Marketing

YouTube tutorials, TikTok demos, Pinterest pins — highest-converting channels for digital products.

Bundle Economics

"Complete Sports Pool Kit" at $29 outsells individual $5 tools. Bundles = 3× revenue per customer.

White-Label Licensing

200 bowling alleys × $500/yr = $100K ARR. Near-zero marginal cost at scale.

The Key Insight

The Market Proved the Demand.
HTML Proves the Delivery Method.

People will pay for anything that saves them 30 minutes of setup time and looks good doing it. Serverless HTML files do everything existing templates do — and more. They're interactive, private, free to host, work offline, and lock no one into an ecosystem.

The opportunity is in connecting the two.
