Future Architecture

Standalone first.
Connected later.

Every tool works perfectly alone. The long game is a protocol that lets HTML documents discover each other.

1

Build the library

Dozens of tools under unified design. Each a standalone .html file.

2

Build recognition

Design language becomes the signal. People recognize the ecosystem.

3

Add the protocol

Doccoin or equivalent — documents link, reference, and transact.

4

Revenue emerges

Brand, library, and network are the asset. Monetization without force.
